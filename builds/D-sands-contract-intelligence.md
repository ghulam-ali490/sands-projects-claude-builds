# Build D — `sands-contract-intelligence`

**Contracts and DA conditions, decoded.** Point it at a 60-page PDF, get the three dates that matter on the calendar.

## Business problem
Development runs on contracts and approvals: building contracts, consultant agreements, off-the-plan sales contracts, JV deeds, DA conditions, insurances. Key dates and special conditions get buried in long documents, and a missed condition or sunset date is a direct, expensive hit.

## What we build
A document-intelligence skill. Point it at a document in the store and it extracts parties, key dates, payment and progress milestones, obligations on S&S, sunset and termination clauses, and unusual or risky terms, then lands the time-critical items on the calendar with reminders.

## Surface
Claude skill over the existing document store, with calendar write (approval-gated).

## Reuses
Document storage (SharePoint or Dropbox) and calendar tools already in place.

## Why it matters
It removes the manual read of every long contract and approval notice, and it makes the dangerous dates impossible to miss.
